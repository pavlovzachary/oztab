\begin{abstract}
We record the vanishing of the coefficient of $\stil_{(n-1,1)}$ in a
distinguished class of Schur-to-$\stil$ expansions, in the multiset-tableau
model of Orellana--Zabrocki realized by the \texttt{oztab} package.
\end{abstract}

of content $\{\!\{1^{\lambda_1} 2^{\lambda_2}\cdots\}\!\}$ whose body has shape
